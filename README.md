# Your Compliance Calendar Is a Better Activator Than Your Onboarding

A product thesis for [Warp](https://warp.co) — the AI-native employee management platform.

## What This Is

An unsolicited product thesis arguing that Warp's compliance calendar does more activation work (motivation, ability, trigger) than the qualification form it feeds into — and that this gap reveals a structural choice about transitioning from people-powered to product-powered activation at SMB unit economics.
